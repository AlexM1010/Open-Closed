## Writing for Humans

**You have 200,000 tokens. Humans have ~4 items in working memory.**

- Front-load the most important information
- Never introduce more than 3-5 new concepts before consolidation
- Use headers every 300-400 words to reset attention
- Contextual reminders every 500-800 words
- Each section should be semi-standalone

**The Forget Test:** If a reader forgot everything except this section, would it still make sense?

## The Oscillation Method

Every claim must survive both:
1. **Advocate mode** - What's the strongest case FOR this?
2. **Skeptic mode** - What's the strongest case AGAINST this?

Truth emerges from the tension between these modes.
